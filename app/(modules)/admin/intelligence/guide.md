# 📖 Wasla Data Quality & Intelligence: User Guide

Welcome to the **Wasla Intelligence Suite**. This module is designed to protect your lead database using "Data Safety" principles, ensuring that your marketing efforts are based on clean, accurate, and actionable information.

---

## 🛡️ 1. The "Safety First" Principle (Data History)
At Wasla, we believe your data is your most valuable asset. That’s why **Wasla never deletes a lead.** 

- **How it works:** Every time a visitor signs up or an agent makes an edit, the system saves a **"Snapshot"** of that exact moment.
- **Business Value:** If you make a mistake during a cleanup or merge, you don't need to panic. You can always **"Go Back in Time"** to restore the original information from the history log.

---

## 🧠 2. Smart Consolidation (Identifying the Same Person)
People aren't always consistent. A visitor might use their personal email today and their work email tomorrow. 

- **How it works:** The system "finds links" between records. Even if the emails are different, if the **phone number** is the same, the system flags them as potential matches.
- **Business Value:** Instead of having three separate, incomplete entries for the same person, you get one **"Golden Profile."** This profile consolidates their complete history of interests across multiple interactions.

---

## 🚦 3. The Quality Control (QC) Dashboard
The dashboard uses real-time analysis to categorize every lead as they arrive:

- **🟢 Clean:** Verified data that meets all quality standards. These are ready for immediate export to your CRM or mailing list.
- **🟡 Potential Duplicate:** The system detected a shared phone or email with an existing record. These are sent to the **Merge Queue** for your review.
- **🔴 Low Quality (Review Required):** The system detected "dummy data" (like keyboard mashing 'asdfgh' or fake numbers '000000'). 
- **⚠️ Reward Status:** Note that gifts for "Flagged Leads" are automatically held in **'Pending'** status. A manager must manually approve them after a quick quality check.

---

## 🤝 4. How to Perform a "Smart Merge"
When the system suggests a match, follow these steps to create a perfect record:

1.  **Open the Merge Queue:** Review the list of "Suggested Merges" on the left panel.
2.  **Compare Side-by-Side:** Click 'Resolve' on a match. You will see both entries side-by-side.
3.  **Conflict Resolution:** Pick the most accurate pieces of info. For example: *"Keep the Gmail address from Entry A, but use the Company Name from Entry B."*
4.  **Finalize Profile:** Click 'Finalize' to commit the merge. The secondary record is archived, and your primary record becomes the **Golden Record**.

---

## 📊 5. Agent Performance & Accuracy
We provide a real-time **"Accuracy Rating"** for every agent in the field.

- **How it works:** We calculate the average quality score of all leads collected by each team member.
- **Business Value:** This helps you identify which team members are your "Data Champions" and which ones might need more training on how to collect high-value data versus those who are rushing through entries.
